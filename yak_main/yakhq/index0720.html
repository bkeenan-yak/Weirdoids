What is Yak?

Yak is the social headquarters for connected kids (and the young at heart too). Every user has the ability to create his or her own unique yak experience. Play games, read stories, enter contests, share yak-tivity with friends, discover, learn and most importantly, have fun!

Yak's Mission

To create a fun, safe and exciting ecosystem for delivering innovative apps to kids and young adults across all digital devices.

Why Yak?

Having fun in a safe way is the most important factor for parents with kids online. Yak is a COPPA compliant social gaming network, with features specifically designed to ensure the safety of our youngest users. Parents are in charge of every social and financial decision, without interrupting the child's play.

Yak is a community of connected kids

The safe place to graze.

We let kids play, connect, learn and share with friends - all in a safe way. As a parent, you control and approve all the content your kid creates, so you can be sure they are safe, and encourage their creativity.

Learn more about parenting on Yak, or sign up with your kid right now!

How it Works

Connect

Log into YakHQ on the web or mobile, and dive in!

Discover

Browse apps of all kinds and start playing.

Share

Express your creativity and share activity with your friends.

Parent Dashboard

Parent accounts have their own control panel to follow activity, approve content and monitor the kids' account.

Parents in Control

Every kid's Yak account is associated with a parent, so you can have a dashboard view of your child's activities. We make it impossible to enter any personal information, so you never have to worry that your kids will reveal personal info. Parents approve all communication happening from a child's account, including friends, sharing, comments and downloads.
